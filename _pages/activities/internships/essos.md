### Project leader:
Rogerio Jorge, (rogerio.jorge@wisc.edu)

### Project description: 
The ESSOS team intends to design the next generation of fusion energy devices.
Using the open-source code [ESSOS](https://github.com/uwplasma/ESSOS), we intend
to show that both coil optimization and magnetic field optimization can be
performed using python-based software such as JAX and DIFFRAX. The goal is
to generate simplified coils and equilibria able to confine alpha particles
stemming from fusion reactions.

The intern would help the ESSOS team to integrate all of the core modules of
ESSOS into abstract classes in order to simplify the optimization procedure.
Furthermore, the intern would help find more efficient optimization strategies
to find better performing fusion devices.

### Intern needs:
The intern will work on scientific Python libraries, such as Spicy, numpy, and
JAX, so knowledge of those would be preferred. Furthermore, basic calculus and
statistics would be a plus, given the optimization character of the projects.
Physics is not necessary; only a minimal amount will be given at the beginning
of the project, which is not crucial for its completion.
